About Meisha

Turning Everyday Pieces Into Statement Looks

Hi, I'm Meisha, the creative mind behind Patches Please! Based right here in Mobile, AL, I built this business because I believe your style should be as unique as you are.

It's simple: come on in, browse the patches, and we'll put together a custom design just for you. Once you've picked your favorites, I use a professional heat press to attach them to your clothing, shoes, handbags, and more. Want to take it up a notch? We can add chains and pins to hats and other items too.

No appointments, no shipping, no waiting. Everything is done on site so you walk out with your finished piece the same day.

  • 🧵 Pick your own patches for a one-of-a-kind design
  • 🔥 Professional heat press for lasting, durable results
  • ✨ Chains, pins & embellishments available for hats and more
  • 👟 Clothing, shoes, handbags and more
  • ⚡ Done on site, same day, every time
